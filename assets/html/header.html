Do you know the value of automating your repetitive tasks?

Over 40% of workers surveyed spend at least a quarter of their work week on manual, repetitive tasks, with email, data collection, and data entry occupying the most time.

This app is designed to highlight the value that you're commiting to repetitive tasks in your workplace.

Think of a single process that your team carries out on a regular basis over the course of a week. By inputting the common position of the person carrying out the task and the number of hours this process takes each week, we can qauntify the potential time saving and the value generated by automating this task.
